chinese-support Anki2 add-on

Addon for Anki2 that may do some of the things that batterseapower’s pinyin-toolkit did for Anki1.

Chinese support

This is a very basic, work-in-progress add-on for Anki 2 to help learners of Chinese. Of the many features of the Anki 1 pinyin toolkit plugin, at the moment there are only one or two working a little bit.

Help wanted

I do not learn (or speak) Chinese and am not relly interessted in this add-on. Any programmer that does is more than welcome to fork this repo and take over development.

Until then, i will probably integrate pull requests sent.

One specific bit of help that non-programmers can offer is to send a nicely formated Anki 2 deck for learning Chinese. The ideal way would be to write it up as a standard model in the file, but just a packaged deck a (.apkg-file) would be helpful, too.

Reading generation

Automatically generate readings when adding cards.

What does work, with an accuracy that i am not qualified to judge, is the genertion of reading (pinyin) for hanzi entered. This works quite similar to the creation of Japanese readings with the Japanese support add-on.
